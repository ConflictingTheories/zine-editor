THE FORGE MEETING
Once upon a shadowed time, long before mortals learned to count the stars or name the seasons, the gods kept the most precious treasures locked away in the vaults of Olympus. Among them was a secret flame, brighter and more alive than any star: a Titaness whose name no tongue yet dared speak aloud.
She was born of the first chaos, sister to the winds and kin to the deep earth-rumble, yet she took the form of a maiden wreathed in living gold. Her hair flowed like molten rivers, her skin shimmered with embers that never burned out, and her eyes held the restless hunger of a storm about to break.
She was called only Fire, and she was wild, untamed, the very heartbeat of wanting and becoming. Passion lived in her breath, curiosity sparked in her fingertips, chaos danced in every flicker. Without her, the world was dim and still; with her, it could blaze into wonder or ruin.
